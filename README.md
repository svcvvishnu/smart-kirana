Smart Stock & Sales Manager
1. Introduction
Smart Stock & Sales Manager is a mobile-first application designed to help small vendors and shop owners manage inventory, sales, customers, and finances efficiently. The app provides real-time stock tracking, automated profit calculation, customer insights, and business analytics to enable data-driven decision-making.

2. Target Users
Bakery owners

Vegetable and fruit vendors

General store owners

Small retail shop owners

Individual sellers

3. User Roles
3.1 Seller – Operations Account (Billing)
Separate login credentials for shop counter usage

Used by cashier or staff

Create and manage bills

Apply discounts

Sell items and auto-reduce stock

Send bills via WhatsApp or Email

Restricted access: no profit, analytics, or personal finance visibility

3.2 Seller – Owner Account (Business + Personal)
Separate login credentials for seller/owner

Combines Business & Stock Account and Personal Account

Manage products and stock

Increase or adjust stock quantities

Track business revenue, expenses, and profits

View analytics dashboards (subscription-based)

View most-selling items, most-profitable items, and valuable customers

Track personal income and expenses separately from business data

Configure alerts and notifications

3.3 Support Account
Acts as customer helpline

Handle seller support tickets

View seller profile and subscription plan

Limited, read-only access to seller data

Escalate issues to Admin

3.4 Admin Account
Onboard sellers

Create and manage subscription plans

Assign subscriptions to sellers

Control feature visibility based on subscription

Manage seller account status

Oversee support operations
